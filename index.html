Project overview

Aim

This project aims to design and develop a simulated cryptocurrency trading platform that provides users with a secure and comprehensive environment for practicing and enhancing trading skills. The platform integrates core functions such as user account management, wallet tracking, and transaction processing, allowing users to customize virtual fund allocations and conduct realistic simulated transactions. Additionally, the platform offers market data visualization, including historical and real-time price charts and trading activity tracking, helping users make informed decisions based on market conditions. Furthermore, trading strategies can be tested using backtesting capabilities, which include selecting historical data, defining strategies, simulating execution, and evaluating results to further assist users in optimizing their trading decisions.。

One of the platform's key features is an AI-driven education module that acts as an intelligent assistant during the trading process. By analyzing historical data and user behavior, it evaluates decisions, offers suggestions to optimize strategies, and identifies areas for improvement. The module also analyzes potential profit and loss probabilities, suggests alternative trading options, and checks if the user's actions align with market trends.

Overall, the platform provides multilingual support and leverages technology, data analytics, and financial education to help users better understand market trends, enhance trading confidence, and continuously optimize trading strategies in a low-risk environment.
